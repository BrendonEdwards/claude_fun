### MTD Compliance Approach

**Xero Simple:**
Xero Simple includes direct HMRC submission capability — you submit your quarterly updates from within Xero itself, with no separate bridging software step. Xero is HMRC-recognised and has been used by UK businesses for VAT filing for years. The MTD for Income Tax support was built specifically for the April 2026 deadline.

**QuarterlyUK:**
QuarterlyUK generates HMRC-aligned quarterly summaries that you export as a CSV and submit via HMRC-approved bridging software. It does not submit directly to HMRC — yet. Direct HMRC submission is on the QuarterlyUK roadmap.

**The practical difference:** Xero's all-in-one approach is simpler. QuarterlyUK's two-step process (record → export → submit via bridging software) adds a step but remains fully MTD-compatible.

### Features: The Differences That Matter

#### Invoice Generation

**QuarterlyUK:** Unlimited professional invoices with no monthly cap.

**Xero Simple:** Limited to 10 invoices per month. If you issue more than 10 invoices in a month, you need to upgrade to a higher Xero plan.

**Verdict: QuarterlyUK** — for freelancers with high invoice volumes, the 10/month cap is a real constraint.

#### Receipt and Expense Capture

**QuarterlyUK:** Manual expense entry using HMRC's 14 categories.

**Xero Simple:** Includes Hubdoc — a receipt capture feature where you photograph a receipt and it is automatically imported and categorised.

**Verdict: Xero Simple** — Hubdoc is a genuine time-saver for busy sole traders with lots of receipts.

#### Bank Feeds

**Xero Simple:** Connects directly to your bank and automatically imports transactions for categorisation.

**QuarterlyUK:** No bank feed integration. You enter expenses manually.

**Verdict: Xero Simple** — automatic bank import removes a significant amount of manual data entry.

#### Mobile App

**Xero Simple:** Full-featured iOS and Android apps.

**QuarterlyUK:** Browser-based. Works on mobile browsers but no dedicated app.

**Verdict: Xero Simple**

#### Invoice Cap

**Xero Simple:** 10 invoices per month — after which you must upgrade.

**QuarterlyUK:** No invoice cap.

**Verdict: QuarterlyUK** — for freelancers who invoice regularly, having no limit is a meaningful advantage.

#### Data Privacy

**Xero Simple:** Data is stored on Xero's cloud servers (New Zealand-founded company, global infrastructure). Xero is GDPR-compliant.

**QuarterlyUK:** Data is stored locally in your browser. No server holds your financial data. Nothing leaves your device.

For sole traders working with sensitive client information, or those who prefer local storage as a matter of principle, this is a material difference. QuarterlyUK's approach means there is no third-party server to breach.

**Verdict: QuarterlyUK** (for privacy-first users)

#### Xero Ecosystem

A significant advantage of Xero Simple is that it sits within the Xero ecosystem. If your business grows and you need payroll, VAT filing, or accountant collaboration, you can upgrade your Xero plan without switching software. Many UK accountants work natively in Xero.

QuarterlyUK does not have an equivalent ecosystem.

**Verdict: Xero Simple** (for users who anticipate growth or work with Xero-native accountants)

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Is Xero Simple available in the UK?**
Yes. Xero Simple was launched specifically for the UK market, targeting sole traders and landlords facing the April 2026 MTD for Income Tax deadline. It is available at [xero.com/uk/pricing-plans/simple/](https://www.xero.com/uk/pricing-plans/simple/).

**What happens if I need to issue more than 10 invoices per month on Xero Simple?**
You would need to upgrade to Xero's Grow plan (£24/month + VAT), which removes the invoice cap and adds additional features. QuarterlyUK does not have an invoice limit.

**Can I switch from Xero Simple to QuarterlyUK?**
Yes — export your financial data from Xero, then begin tracking in QuarterlyUK from the date you switch. Historical data would not be directly importable, but all new transactions can be managed in QuarterlyUK.

**Does QuarterlyUK work on mobile?**
QuarterlyUK is browser-based and works in mobile browsers. There is no dedicated iOS or Android app (this is on the roadmap).
