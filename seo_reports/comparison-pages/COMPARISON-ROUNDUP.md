### Frequently Asked Questions

**Is there free MTD software in the UK?**
Yes. Clear Books and Sage Individual both offer free plans that are HMRC-recognised and support direct quarterly submission to HMRC. FreeAgent is also free if you bank with NatWest, Royal Bank of Scotland, or Ulster Bank.

**What is the cheapest paid MTD software in the UK?**
As of February 2026, QuarterlyUK at £2.50/month is the cheapest paid MTD tool available. The next cheapest paid option is QuickBooks Sole Trader at £6/month inc. VAT (post-introductory).

**Do I need HMRC-recognised software?**
From April 2026, sole traders with income over £50,000 must use MTD-compatible software that can submit quarterly updates to HMRC — either directly or via CSV export to approved bridging software. HMRC maintains a list of recognised tools at [gov.uk/guidance/find-software-thats-compatible-with-making-tax-digital-for-income-tax](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/find-software-thats-compatible-with-making-tax-digital-for-income-tax).

**Does QuarterlyUK submit directly to HMRC?**
Not directly — it generates quarterly summaries in CSV format which you submit to HMRC via approved bridging software. Direct HMRC submission is on the QuarterlyUK product roadmap.

**What is bridging software?**
Bridging software is an HMRC-recognised tool that acts as the connection between your record-keeping software (like QuarterlyUK) and HMRC's systems. You export a CSV from QuarterlyUK and import it into the bridging software to submit your quarterly update.
