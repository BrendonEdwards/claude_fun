## Executive Summary

- **The digital product market on Gumroad/LemonSqueezy is dominated by Notion templates, AI prompts, courses, and SaaS boilerplates.** Price sweet spots cluster at $27--$97 for templates/tools and $149--$299 for SaaS boilerplates. Typical conversion rates for paid products range 1--10% (median ~3--7% for optimised pages). Platform fees range from 4%+$0.40 (Polar) to 10%+$0.50 (Gumroad) per transaction, plus payment processing.
- **The Next.js SaaS boilerplate market is crowded (70+ tracked competitors) but proven to generate significant revenue.** ShipFast (Marc Lou) generated $528K in its first 4 months at a $199--$299 price point. However, latecomer boilerplates see far lower revenue. Underserved gaps exist in niche-vertical boilerplates (e.g., AI-specific starters, onboarding toolkits, industry-vertical templates).
- **AI-wrapper micro-SaaS tools show strong traction for solo founders.** The micro-SaaS market is projected to grow from $15.7B to $59.6B by 2030. Median profitable micro-SaaS earns ~$4.2K MRR. ~70% of micro-SaaS products earn under $1K MRR, but ~95% reach profitability within year one due to low overhead.
